Things To Remember For A Diabetic

DO'S DON'TS
1.Check your blood sugar regularly. 1.Avoid underground vegetables e.g. sweet potato, beetroot, pumpkin, etc.
2.Exercise daily for at least 30 mins. 2.Patients who are on insulin or oral hypoglycaemic agents should not fast.
3.Sleep for minimum 6 hours daily. 3.Alcohol increases blood pressure and triglycerides and hence weakens the heart.
4.Keep yourself hydrated. 4.Avoid eating fatty cuts of meat and processed or frozen meat.
5.Include salads and high fibre food in your diet. 5.Don’t go for white bread, white rice, deep fried foods and sweets.
6.Go for skinless chicken and lean cut meats instead of red meats. (It is best to be vegetarian.) 6.Don’t go overboard on servings of fruits and avoid processed fruits in the tins.
7.Eat more whole wheat bread, roti’s, brown rice and oats. 7.Reduce saturated and partially hydrogenated fats and oil.
8.Have small servings of apple, bananas and oranges, etc. 8.Avoid aerated and caffeinated drinks and artificial sweeteners.
